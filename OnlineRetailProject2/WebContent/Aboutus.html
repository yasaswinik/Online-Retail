ABOUT US

We'are a British multinational grocery and general merchandise retailer headquartered in United Kingdom.It is the third largest retailer in the world measured by profits and second-largest retailer in the world measured by revenues. It has stores in 12 countries across Asia and Europe and is the grocery market leader in the UK (where it has a market share of around 30%), the Republic of Ireland, Hungary Malaysia and Thailand.
